About CoCSC

The Center of Culture and Student Creativity

Located in room 312-F, the CoCSC serves as the creative nucleus of NUPP — a structural subdivision dedicated to nurturing talent across all faculties. We provide every individual with the opportunity for creative self-realization, running extracurricular activities almost every week of the academic year.

Our Creative Ensembles

01

Folk & Ballad Dance Groups

Dance

Our folk dance ensemble preserves and performs traditional Ukrainian choreography, representing NUPP at regional and national competitions. Beginners welcome — no prior experience required.

Open to all years · Rehearsals: Mon & Wed 18:00
02

Pop Dance Ensemble & Vocal Group

Vocal

Contemporary dance fused with vocal performance. This ensemble regularly tours regional festivals and has won multiple awards at all-Ukrainian student cultural competitions.

Auditions each September · Rehearsals: Tue & Thu 17:30
03

Chapel "Kredo" — University Choir

Music

The university's flagship choral ensemble performing at commemorative ceremonies, cultural galas, and public concerts throughout the academic year. Known for their solemn patriotic repertoire.

Mixed voices welcome · Rehearsals: Fri 17:00
04

University Brass Band

Music

The brass band performs at major university ceremonies including graduation, national holidays, and official public events in Poltava city. Instrument lessons provided for new members.

Wind/percussion players · Contact CoCSC
05

Theater & Circus Studio

Theater

From dramatic productions to acrobatic performances, this studio pushes the boundaries of student creativity. Annual theatrical showcase held each spring semester.

Open casting · Workshops every Saturday 11:00
06

School of KVN Teams

Comedy

The only KVN school in the entire Poltava region. We train students in improv comedy, scriptwriting, and stage performance — preparing them for inter-university and national KVN leagues.

All faculties compete · Tryouts: October
PK
Poltavskyi Kray
Featured Ensemble

"Poltavskyi Kray" — Song and Dance Ensemble

The crown jewel of NUPP's cultural life, the "Poltavskyi Kray" (Poltava Land) ensemble has performed Ukrainian folk songs and dances for generations of students. They have represented the university at international festivals and are a source of enormous institutional pride.

Step 2

Step 3
