Facilities

Campus Amenities

Everything you need is right here — no need to travel far.

01

High-Speed Internet

Wired and Wi-Fi access in every dormitory room, common areas, and study rooms. Available 24/7 at no extra cost.

02

Two Gymnasiums

Fully equipped sports facilities open to all campus residents, with supervised group sessions and open gym hours.

03

Sports Grounds

Outdoor football pitches, basketball courts, and athletic areas maintained for year-round use by dormitory teams.

04

Canteen

A large student canteen serving hot meals, snacks, and beverages at subsidized prices for all enrolled students.

05

Student Shop

On-site convenience store stocking daily essentials, stationery, and basic groceries — no need to leave campus.

06

Hairdresser

Campus hairdressing salon offering affordable cuts and styling services for students and staff.

07

Health Center

On-campus health center with general practitioners and counseling services. Psychological support available for international students.

08

Library

Access to the university's extensive library resources, study rooms, and digital academic databases — open during all exam periods.

Dormitory Events

Life in the Dorms

Life in the dormitories goes far beyond just sleeping and studying. A rich calendar of in-house events keeps the community vibrant and connected.

October

Halloween Celebration

An international favourite — dormitory teams compete for best-decorated common room and most creative costumes in a campus-wide Halloween event.

November

International Students' Day

A celebration of NUPP's global community — cultural evenings, food from 20+ countries, and performances by international student groups.

March

Shevchenko Days

Honoring Ukraine's greatest poet, Taras Shevchenko — poetry readings, art exhibitions, and special evening programs across all dormitories.

Year-round

Table Tennis Leagues

Ongoing table tennis competitions within and between dormitories, encouraging friendly rivalry and physical activity throughout the year.

May

Campus Cup Football

The biggest sporting event on campus — five dormitory teams battle for the Campus Cup mini-football championship.

Ongoing

Cultural Competition

Inter-dormitory cultural competitions judged by a student panel — music, performance, decoration, and community spirit all count.
