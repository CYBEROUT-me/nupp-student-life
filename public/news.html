Peacebuilding Initiatives and Intercultural Dialogue: Future Translator Participated in an Erasmus+ Youth Exchange in Italy!

Yelizavieta Samoilenko, a third-year philology student at Poltava Polytechnic, participated in the Erasmus+ "Peace and Youth" exchange program held March 2–6, 2026, in Ceprano, Italy. The initiative brought together young people from Ukraine, France, Italy, and Poland to explore themes of war, peace, and democratic values.

The curriculum included workshops on democratic principles, historical discussions centered on World War II, and a field trip to Cassino and the historic Monte Cassino monastery. Cultural evenings featured presentations of national traditions, with the Ukrainian delegation sharing their country's modern challenges alongside traditional cultural offerings.

"This project was not only an opportunity to travel for me but also a valuable experience of communicating with people from different countries," Samoilenko reflected. Participants engaged in daily sessions concluding at 4:30 PM, allowing time for cultural exploration and experiencing Italian cuisine.
