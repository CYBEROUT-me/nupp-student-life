Inter-Faculty KVN Championship — Final Round

The grand finale of the university's most popular event. Eight faculty teams compete with original scripts, musical acts, and improvisational humor. The School of KVN — the only one of its kind in the Poltava region — presents its graduating class this year.

Victory Day Remembrance Concert

An annual commemoration featuring Chapel "Kredo" choir, the university brass band, and student soloists. Honoring NUPP's 267 staff and students who served in WWII, including D.M. Batishchev who raised the Soviet victory flag in Berlin.

🕕 16:00 📍 University Square 🎟 Free — outdoor Memorial

Campus Cup — Inter-Dormitory Mini-Football

Annual mini-football tournament between all five dormitories. Teams represent their dorms in a knockout bracket, with finals and awards ceremony in the evening. Previous champions: Dormitory 3 "Eagles".

🕕 10:00–18:00 📍 Campus Sports Ground 🎟 Free spectator entry Sport

Erasmus+ Info Day — Study Abroad Opportunities

The International Relations Office (room 213-C) presents open exchange program slots with universities in Austria, Germany, Poland, and Italy. Past participants will share their experiences and tips for successful applications.
